FISH'S

Jenkinsons Aquariums typically have a diverse collection of fish species, including tropical fish, reef fish, and predatory fish like sharks and rays.

Seal:Noelani

Commonly featured in aquariums, seals are charismatic marine mammals. They are known for their playful behavior and graceful swimming.

Sharks:

Some aquariums have large tanks with various species of sharks. These exhibits are designed to educate visitors about these apex predators.

Turtles:

Sea turtles are often featured in aquariums as they are endangered species. They provide educational opportunities about conservation efforts.

Jellyfish:

Jellyfish exhibits can be mesmerizing, with their translucent bodies and graceful movements. They are usually showcased in tanks with specialized lighting.

Anemones:

Sea anemones are carnivorous marine animals known for their stinging tentacles, which they use to capture prey. They often form mutualistic relationships with clownfish in coral reefs..

Sea starfish:

jenkinson Starfish, also known as sea stars, are marine animals characterized by their radial symmetry and distinctive arms. They use tube feet to move and feed on small marine organisms

Octopus:

Octopuses are highly intelligent, eight-armed cephalopods known for their problem-solving abilities and camouflage skills. They are carnivorous and have a unique, beak-like mouth for capturing prey.

Sea Hores:

jenkinson Seahorses are unique marine fish with upright bodies, a curled tail, and a distinctive appearance.

clownFish

Orange clownfish are a famous specie striking coloration and mutualistic relationship with sea anemones.

LionFish:

Lionfish are venomous marine fish known for their striking appearance, featuring colorful fin rays and a fan-like display.

Blue Tang:

Blue tang fish, recognized for their vibrant blue color and surgeon's scalpel-like tail, inhabit coral reefs and are popular in the aquarium trade.

Whale Shark:

Whale sharks are the world's largest fish, with distinctive spotted patterns, and they are gentle filter-feeders often found in warm oceans.

Harp Seal:

Giant Manta Ray:

Giant manta rays are large, graceful oceanic rays known for their impressive wingspans and filter-feeding habits.

Goblin Shark:

Jenkinson Aquariums Goblin sharks are rare deep-sea sharks with unique, elongated snouts and pinkish-gray skin.
